Overview

Avviare Educational Hub was introduced to promote excellence in academic, career and technical studies in order to prepare students to enter and remain competitive in a global workforce. AEH is registered under the society name THE FUTURE AHEAD FOUNDATION, under the REGISTRATION ACT XXI of 1860, registration no. 57371/2006 in the year 2006.
